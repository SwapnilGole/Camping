About Alibaug Beach Camping

Be the first to search for buried treasure — The ocean is full of surprises and secrets. Beach camping lets you wake up, step out of your tent, and have first dibs on everything that washed ashore while you slept—sand dollars, starfish, sea glass, buried treasure…

Build yourself a castle—Who needs a high rise hotel when you can be an architect and builder of your very own castle, complete with moat? You can HGTV-it however you’d like, but we don’t recommend sleeping in it.

Watch epic sunrises and sunsets—Any sunset you can watch outside is a great sunset. But not much beats the open expanse of the sun rising or setting over the water.

Day 1

Arrive at the Campsite

3:00 PM: Arrive at the Alibaug camping site to begin your offbeat beach camping experience

4:30 PM: You will be given a complimentary welcome beverage with snacks once you have checked in and have freshened up.

6:00 PM : Settle down at beach and enjoy sea breeze with sunset view with your love ones .

7:00 PM Enjoy the scrumptious Barbecue session at the beachside (Veg/Non-veg).

8:00 PM Once the evening settles in, you can play some recreational indoor-outdoor games, enjoy around the warm and cozy campfire, with stories and music to accompany you.

9:00 PM Dinner will be served here, with the options of both veg and non-veg unlimited buffet.

10:30 PM Enjoy your open air movie screening session and head back to your tents as per your convenience.
